Transform 'file.atlas' into a text atlas:
#$ packed-to-text file.atlas
Transform all atlases on your desktop into text atlases:
#$ packed-to-text ~/Desktop/*.atlas
Transform all atlases in your home folder to text atlases, and save
them to a folder on your desktop
#$ packed-to-text ~/*.atlas ~/Desktop/my-folder
Transform an atlas to GeoJSON, and fail fast if it does not exist:
#$ packed-to-text file.atlas -gs
