Convert a PBF to a raw Atlas:
#$ pbf2raw 6-42-24.pbf --countryName KAZ
Convert a PBF to a raw Atlas with a custom output directory:
#$ pbf2raw 6-42-24.pbf --countryName KAZ --output=rawAtlas
Convert a directory of PBFs to raw Atlases in a custom output directory:
#$ pbf2raw pbf/* --countryName KAZ --output=rawAtlas
